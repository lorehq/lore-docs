## The Five Primitives
Lore organizes agent intelligence into five canonical types:
1. **Rules** — Behavioral constraints and standards.
2. **Skills** — Procedural commands and capabilities.
3. **Agents** — Roles and delegation patterns.
4. **Fieldnotes** — Machine-global environmental memory.
5. **Runbooks** — Adaptive multi-step procedures.
